Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our partnership model and delivery.

What is the typical minimum contract length?

We require a minimum commitment of three months. This duration is necessary to complete the Discovery phase, execute the campaign launch, and gather sufficient data for meaningful optimization and scale. We don't do short-term testing.

Do you only work in Hawassa, or nationwide?

Our headquarters are in Hawassa, but our strategic reach is nationwide. We manage digital campaigns targeted at any major city or region in Ethiopia, and our production team can travel for essential cinematic shoots.

Who owns the creative assets (videos, graphics)?

Upon successful completion of the agreed-upon contract term, your company retains full ownership and licensing rights to all creative assets produced on your behalf.

How do we track performance?

We provide weekly performance reports that focus on key metrics such as ROI, lead quality, and brand reach, presented in a concise dashboard view. We hold monthly strategy review meetings.
